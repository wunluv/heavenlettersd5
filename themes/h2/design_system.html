Design System

The official style guide for the Heavenletters brand. This living document ensures a consistent, serene, and uplifting user experience.

Principles

Serene & Uplifting

The design should evoke a sense of peace, clarity, and spiritual upliftment, creating a sanctuary for readers.

Clear & Accessible

Content is paramount. The design must prioritize readability and be accessible to all, regardless of ability.

Light & Spacious

Generous use of white space creates a calm, uncluttered environment that allows the words to breathe.
